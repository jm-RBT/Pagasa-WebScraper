## DIFFERENT SELECTION PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: Select Single Word
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│ Tropical ┏━━━━━━━┓ PEPITO   │
│         ┃Cyclone┃           │
│         ┗━━━━━━━┛           │
└─────────────────────────────┘
Result: "Cyclone"

### Pattern 2: Select Line
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┓ │
│ ┃ Wind Speed: 150 km/h (Category 2)     ┃ │
│ ┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┛ │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
Result: "Wind Speed: 150 km/h (Category 2)"

### Pattern 3: Select Partial Text
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Location: ┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━┓ Manila, Luzon    │
│           ┃60 km NE of ┃                   │
│           ┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━┛                   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
Result: "60 km NE of "

### Pattern 4: Select Multiple Lines
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┓ │
│ ┃ Location: 60 km NE of Manila, Luzon   ┃ │

│ ┃ Movement: West-Northwest at 20 km/h   ┃ │
│ ┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┛ │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
Result: "Location: 60 km NE of Manila, Luzon 
         Wind Speed: 150 km/h (Category 2) 
         Movement: West-Northwest at 20 km/h"

## COMPARISON: OLD VS NEW

╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║ OLD: Copy All Page Text                                       ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

[📋 Copy Text] → Copies entire page
                 ↓
"SEVERE WEATHER BULLETIN #03
Tropical Cyclone PEPITO (KAREN)
Advisory No. 15
Location: 60 km NE of Manila, Luzon
Wind Speed: 150 km/h (Category 2)
Movement: West-Northwest at 20 km/h
... (ALL text on page)"

❌ No control over what gets copied
❌ Must manually edit after pasting
❌ Copies headers, footers, everything

╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║ NEW: Select Specific Text                                     ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

1. Drag over "60 km NE of Manila, Luzon"
2. [📋 Copy Selected]
                 ↓
"60 km NE of Manila, Luzon"

✅ Full control over selection
✅ Copy exactly what you need
✅ No manual editing required
